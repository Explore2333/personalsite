Environmental Issues

Steps You Can Take Today

Use Resuable Products: Carry metal water bottles and reusable shopping bads instead of using plastics or single-use plastics.


Save Water: Turn off the tap when brushing your teeth and take shorter showers.


Conserve Energy: Turn off lights and fans when not using them to reduce energy consumption.


Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Separate recyclable materials from regular waste and reuse items before throwing them away.


Plant Trees: Plant or take care of a tree. Trees help with air quality and provide habitats for animals around you.
