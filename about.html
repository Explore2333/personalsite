About Us

EcoIndia is a student-led website to raise awareness on environmental issues affecting India. We hope to educate people how simple actions can lead to big changes for a greener and cleaner India. Most importantly, we want to inspire everyone that they can play a part in this goal today.




What We Do

We share information that is easy to understand and actionable!


We inspire students to bring change in their communities by taking small actions for big changes!


We promote awareness about India's environmental challenges and ways to combat them.
