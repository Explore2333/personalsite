Environmental Issues in India Currently

1. Air Pollution

Air Pollution is one of the most serious environmental concerns in India. Smoke coming from vehicles, factories, and crop burning leads to harmful chemicals in the air. This can cause poor air quality, leading to breathing problems and other health issues.


1. Plastic Pollution

Plastic usage is a major challenge to India's environmental. Millions of tons of plastic waste is generated every year and much of it isn't recycled. These plastics often take hundreds of years to fully decompose. Reducing single-use plastics and increasing recycling can help solve this problem.


1. Water Pollution

Water Pollution is another environmental issue in India as many rivers and lakes are polluted by sewage, industrial waste, and plastic. This can affect drinking water quality in India and can spread diseases.
